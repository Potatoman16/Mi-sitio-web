Sergio David Cia Luvecce

I am a PhD student at the Institut de Mathématiques de Jussieu-Paris Rive Gauche of Sorbonne Université under the supervision of Pr. Olivier Dudas, and I am Teaching Assistant (ATER) at the Université d'Aix-Marseille.

My research interest is higher representation theory and its applications to the representation theory of finite groups, Lie algebras and more generally finite dimensional algebras. The goal of my research is to contribute to the development of these representation theories, unraveling their structure and finding the interactions between them.
